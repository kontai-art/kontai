What We Do

We build interactive spaces that make complex information tangible. We use light, motion, sound and live data to turn invisible systems into something visitors can understand, remember and act on.

Energy & Infrastructure

Real-time visualisations of power generation, grid load and energy flow. People can “see” how the system works in seconds.

Climate & Oceans

Spaces where climate data becomes light, colour and sound. Science turns into an emotional experience people don’t forget.

Innovation & Industry

Portable “touch the system” showrooms for leadership, partners and investors. Instead of slides, they try it themselves.

Sustainability & ESG

Live-impact walls that show CO₂, efficiency and recycling data. Teams and visitors see the impact of their choices in real time.

Selected Projects

Delivered

The Energy Flower (Fingrid)

The national grid operator needed to explain “how electricity moves” to the public, schools, press and decision-makers.

We built a kinetic light sculpture that pulses with live grid data. When demand spikes, the sculpture “breathes” faster. When renewables increase, colour shifts.

Impact: Easier stakeholder talks, calmer energy discussions, longer visitor engagement.

Prototype

Climate & Oceans — Immersive Room

Research teams needed funding support. Charts weren’t creating urgency.

We turned climate and ocean data (temperature, acidity) into a responsive space. The room itself “warms” as the sea warms.

Impact: Funders said “now I finally see it.” Helped open new funding conversations.

Anonymised

Energy Visitor Wall — Utility Centre

The visitor centre felt like homework, and people left confused.

We built a long glowing wall where “light is electricity.” Touch wind → see where it flows. Touch nuclear → follow the path.

Impact: ~2× visitor dwell time. Satisfaction rose from 3.1 → 4.8/5. Guides spend less time reteaching basics.

In Development

Boardroom Demo — “Touch the System”

Leadership had to sign off on a complex platform. Slides weren’t landing.

We built a portable interactive setup for the boardroom. Move your hand — the projected system reacts live.

Impact: Decisions in days instead of weeks. Internal resistance dropped.

Concept Pilot

Sustainability Hub — Live Metrics Wall

“We’re sustainable” wasn’t convincing investors or staff.

We built a wall that changes colour and pattern based on live CO₂, recycling and efficiency data. Teams can literally see their own footprint improving.

Impact: Stronger employer brand. Easier ESG storytelling. Reusable visuals for investor meetings.

Prototype

University Lab — Living Data Sculpture

Researchers needed industry partners to “get it” — without 30 slides of theory.

We built a hanging sculpture that pulses and shifts colour with live research data. When something important happens in the data, you see it.

Impact: Opened partner talks faster. Helped science feel real and fundable.
