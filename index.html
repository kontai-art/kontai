Selected references

One delivered project and selected prototypes / in-development work. In some cases, client name is anonymised but the scenario is real.

Delivered

The Energy Flower (Fingrid)

Finland’s national grid operator wanted to make “energy flow” visible to non-engineers. We turned live grid data into a kinetic light sculpture. People could literally see the system breathing.

Impact: faster, calmer stakeholder conversations.

Prototype

Climate & Oceans — Concept Study

Ocean and climate data is overwhelming. We built an immersive layer-cake of light, depth and movement so partners and sponsors could immediately see: “Here’s what’s changing, here’s why it matters.”

Use case: funding, partnerships, public engagement.

Anonymised

Nordic Utility — Visitor Flow Pilot

The client needed to redesign how visitors move through an energy visitor centre. We mapped attention hotspots, dwell time and confusion points.

Impact: clearer story path → better conversion through the space.

In Development

Embodied Interaction — R&D

Executives can literally “touch” an invisible system — a data platform, an AI model, an infrastructure layer — and see it respond. Ideal for winning senior buy-in fast.

Concept Pilot

Sustainability Hub — Concept Pilot

ESG and sustainability teams struggle to make impact feel real. We turn CO₂, biodiversity or efficiency metrics into an environment you can stand inside — not just look at in a PDF.

Prototype

University Lab — Data Sculpture Prototype

Research partners often don’t “see” the value. We built a physical data sculpture driven by live research feeds, so industry partners could understand in 30 seconds what normally takes a 30-page deck.

FAQ

How long does a pilot take?

Usually 6–8 weeks from first conversation to something people can stand in and react to.

What budgets are we talking about?

Pilots typically start around tens of thousands. Full permanent spaces land in the hundreds of thousands depending on scale, safety and integration.

Where is the data hosted?

EU / Switzerland (Infomaniak). We don't default to invasive tracking. We can generate anonymised impact reports for management and funders.

Do you support after launch?

Yes. You get an operating playbook, SLA options and iterative updates (content, narrative, layout, analytics).
