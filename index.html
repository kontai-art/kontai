What We Do

We design and build interactive spaces that make complex information tangible. Each project combines light, motion, sound and live data — turning ideas into experiences that inform, inspire and convince.

Energy & Infrastructure

Real-time installations that visualize national energy flow and production in motion. Visitors can see how renewable sources interact with the grid — turning technical data into intuitive understanding.

Climate & Oceans

Immersive visualizations for museums and research centers. Light and sound respond to environmental data — translating science into emotional experience that stays with the audience.

Innovation & Industry

Modular showrooms that connect physical objects to real data. Touch, movement or sensors trigger stories — helping teams, investors and clients see progress and value instantly.

Events & Public Engagement

Adaptive installations for fairs, expos and festivals. Designed for scalability and transport — engaging audiences, sponsors and partners through memorable experiences.

Selected Projects

Energy Flower

A live energy visualization developed with Fingrid. The installation turns national grid data into movement, color and rhythm — creating an ambient pulse of the country's energy in real time.

Impact: Enhanced public understanding of renewable balance and energy flow, increased dwell time at exhibitions by +40%.

Sea of Life

Immersive storytelling installation merging marine science and art. Real-time ocean data and soundscapes bring audiences closer to the invisible forces that sustain life underwater.

Impact: Improved visitor engagement metrics, used by researchers and educators for public outreach.

Digital Twin Showroom

Interactive 3D showroom for a technology client. Visitors explore products through touch-based and sensor-driven interfaces — blending physical and digital layers seamlessly.

Impact: Shortened sales cycle, stronger investor communication, and measurable visitor retention growth.
